ATM Transaction & Financial Performance Dashboard
📊 Project Overview
This Power BI dashboard provides a data-driven analysis of ATM operations, focusing on the intersection of maintenance costs, regional revenue, and transactional trends. It enables stakeholders to monitor operational efficiency and identify high-performing regions across North-East India and Northern territories.

🚀 Key Features
Cost Breakdown: Detailed analysis of maintenance drivers including CRA, AMC (ATM/UPS/VSAT), and spare replacements.

Regional Insights: Revenue performance tracking across 11 regions, including Punjab, Assam, and Jammu & Kashmir.

Operational Metrics: Real-time tracking of Average Uptime (91.9%), Gross Profit (59.6%), and EBILL costs.

Trend Analysis: Temporal comparison of Financial vs. Non-Financial transactions to forecast demand.

🛠️ Tech Stack
Tool: Power BI Desktop

Data Processing: Power Query (ETL) for data cleaning and preparation.

Modeling: DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for custom measures like Gross Profit % and Time-Intelligence calculations.

Visuals: Pie charts for cost distribution, bar charts for regional revenue, and line graphs for temporal trends.

📈 Business Impact
Operational Efficiency: Identified primary cost drivers (ATM AMC and CRA) to streamline maintenance budgets.

Strategic Expansion: Highlighted top-performing regions to guide resource allocation.

Predictive Planning: Transaction trends allow for better cash replenishment scheduling, reducing downtime.
